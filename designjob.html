Job Posting - Creative Designer

Creative Designer

Company: Acme Corporation

Location: San Francisco, CA

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Category: Design

Job Description

Acme Corporation is seeking a highly creative and talented Creative Designer to join our design team. As a Creative Designer, you will be responsible for conceptualizing and designing visually appealing and engaging digital and print materials.

Responsibilities include:

  • Creating visually stunning designs for digital platforms, including websites, mobile apps, and social media.
  • Developing and producing creative assets such as graphics, illustrations, and layouts for various marketing collaterals.
  • Collaborating with cross-functional teams to understand project requirements and deliver design solutions that align with the company's brand guidelines.
  • Staying up-to-date with industry trends and incorporating innovative design techniques and technologies into your work.
  • Providing design support for presentations, events, and other promotional materials.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, Visual Communication, or a related field.
  • 3+ years of experience as a creative designer in a professional setting.
  • Proficiency in industry-standard design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign).
  • Strong portfolio showcasing your creative design skills and projects.
  • Solid understanding of design principles, typography, color theory, and layout.
  • Excellent attention to detail and ability to meet project deadlines.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.

How to Apply

To apply for this position, please submit your resume, portfolio, and cover letter to jobs@acmecorp.com.
