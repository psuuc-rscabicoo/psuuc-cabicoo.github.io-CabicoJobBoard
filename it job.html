Job Posting - Information Technology

Information Technology Specialist

Company: Acme Corporation

Location: New York, NY

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Category: Information Technology

Job Description

Acme Corporation is seeking a highly skilled Information Technology Specialist to join our dynamic IT team. As an IT Specialist, you will be responsible for managing and maintaining our company's technology infrastructure, including hardware, software, and network systems.

Responsibilities include:

  • Installing, configuring, and troubleshooting computer hardware, software, and peripherals.
  • Providing technical support to end-users and resolving IT-related issues.
  • Managing network systems, including routers, switches, and firewalls.
  • Performing regular system backups and data recovery.
  • Ensuring network and data security, including implementing appropriate security measures.
  • Collaborating with internal teams to identify and implement technology solutions to enhance business operations.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • 2+ years of experience in an IT support role.
  • Proficiency in troubleshooting hardware, software, and network issues.
  • Strong knowledge of network systems and protocols.
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment.

How to Apply
